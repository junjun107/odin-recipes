Pork Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 (6 ounce) boneless pork loin chop, cut into small pieces
  • ¼ cup chopped carrot
  • ¼ cup chopped broccoli
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup cold cooked rice
  • 1 ½ tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder

Directions

Step 1 Melt butter in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir pork, carrot, broccoli, peas, and green onion in melted butter until pork is cooked through, 7 to 10 minutes. Remove pork mixture to a bowl and return skillet to medium heat.
